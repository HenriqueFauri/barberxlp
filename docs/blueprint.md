# **App Name**: BarberX Hub

## Core Features:

- Informational Pages: Display key information across Home, About, Pricing, and Contact pages to inform and engage potential customers.
- Contact Form: Allow visitors to submit their information and inquiries directly through a contact form that integrates with Firebase Functions.
- Lead Capture: Capture user contact information through the contact form and store it securely in Firestore for follow-up and marketing purposes.
- Responsive Design: Ensure the website is fully responsive, providing an optimal viewing experience across a range of devices, including mobile, tablet, and desktop.
- Animated Transitions: Incorporate subtle animations to improve user experience with tasteful fade, slide, and hover effects using framer-motion.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color (background): Deep dark blue (#030918), creating a premium and modern backdrop.
- Secondary color (internal areas, contrasts): Dark grey (#1A1A1A) for effective contrast and structuring of content.
- Gradient: Use gradient (#030918 to #15203A) on heroes, cards, and CTAs for a visually appealing depth.
- Logo/Title Font: 'Roboto' (sans-serif) at 600 weight, offering a bold and clear presentation of the BarberX name.
- Subtitle Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) at 150 weight, providing a clean and readable secondary text style.
- Primary Text Color: White (#FFFFFF) to ensure readability against the dark background.
- Secondary Text Color: Light grey (#BFBFBF) for less emphasis where needed.
- Simple, solid icons enhance UX without clutter.
- Employ a minimal, modern SaaS aesthetic featuring square shapes with softly rounded corners and abundant white space for visual ease.
- Subtle fade and slide animations add sophistication.
